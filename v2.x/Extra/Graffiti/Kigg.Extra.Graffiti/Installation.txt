Installation Notes
==================

1. Copy Kigg.Extra.Graffiti.dll into the bin directory.
2. Next Login as an Admin
3. Click Presentation -> Themes.
4. Click Personalize.
5. Click your desired template like blog.post.view, post.view etc where the counter will appear.
6. Put $DotNetShoutout.Counter($post) or $DotNetShoutout.Counter($post, 'YOUR_borderColor', 'YOUR_textBackColor', 'YOUR_textForeColor', 'YOUR_countBackColor', 'YOUR_countForeColor') if you want change the default color.
7. Click save changes.
8. Now View the page/post, the counter image will appear.
